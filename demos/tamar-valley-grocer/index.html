Our Story

Your neighbourhood
grocer & canteen

Tamar Valley Grocer is more than a supermarket. We're a local institution — a place to grab your weekly groceries, a fresh barista coffee, or a bite to eat made from ingredients you can trust.

From our deli counter to our canteen, everything we do is guided by a love of good food and a commitment to the Launceston community.

  • Gourmet groceries and local produce
  • Freshly made canteen food every day
  • Barista coffee and sweet treats
  • Afterpay available
